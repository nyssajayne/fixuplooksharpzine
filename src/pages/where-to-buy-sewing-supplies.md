If you’re in New York, the Garment District is just south of Times Square in Manhattan. While not as bustling as it once was, it’s still a great resource and is primarily made up of independent businesses. My top shops include:

1. **SIL Thread** <span class="no-print">\([map](https://maps.app.goo.gl/c18QgKbL7XRisCpd6)\)</span>.  My favourite place for thread and tools, and they have a wide array of high-quality zippers.
2. **Pacific Trimming** <span class="no-print">\([map](https://maps.app.goo.gl/rGcuAYejbv2nVZYU9)\)</span>.  This is a great one-stop for buttons, trim, Rit Dye, embroidery floss, basically any little thing you might need.
3. **Steinlauf and Stoller** <span class="no-print">\([map](https://maps.app.goo.gl/xmb4RE2RmAQCAeZj7)\)</span>.  I really love their button selection here; it’s much smaller than other stores, but they have a lot of vintage deadstock, so you may be able to find something quite special.
4. **Mood Fabrics** <span class="no-print">\([map](https://maps.app.goo.gl/2uGn3dJpxPXftZCt6)\)</span>.  Mood is the closest thing to an all-in-one fabric and haberdashery, so if you were good at navigating Joann's, you’ll be able to handle Mood.



If you need to order online, **[Wawak](https://www.wawak.com)** is an industry favourite.  They have great prices and if you order over $35, express shipping is the default.
