I think it’s difficult to find clothes that don’t completely exploit communities and the environment, but there’s ways to leverage your individual impact in really mindful ways.  The easiest way is to look after the clothes you acquire and rewear them with joy.

## Launder with love
Get your hands on a clothes horse and avoid the tumble dryer.  High heat degrades clothing fibers faster, particularly synthetic fibers.  For all other laundry advice, the r/laundry subreddit is unmatched.

## Tailor to fit
Make friends with your local dry cleaner and get those sleeves taken up and trousers let out.  Clothes that fit properly don’t wear out as fast.  This is also a good chance to pre-mend clothes, like adding denim patches on the inner thighs of jeans.

## Mend early
If you notice a hole or a tear, get onto it straight away, as wear and tear tends to escalate once it starts.

## Rinse and repeat
Learn to love re-wearing your favourite outfits.  I must admit, this takes time, particularly when newness is valued in fashion.  There’s no denying the thrill of an outfit’s debut, but I also look forward to wearing a classic outfit because I know it’s going to make me feel good.
